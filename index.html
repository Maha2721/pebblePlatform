Shape + Grip

The Pebble mouse is designed for the average hand. Your hand is not average. This family of add-ons adapts the shape, grip width, posture support, and tactile feedback of the Pebble to fit you — without modifying the electronics inside.

The Problem

One shape does not fit everyone.

The standard Pebble is designed for a medium-sized right hand in a palm grip. If your hand is larger, smaller, or if you prefer a claw or fingertip grip — the mouse is working against you. Over hours of use, the wrong shape causes fatigue, pain, and loss of precision.

✋

Hand Size Mismatch

Large hands hang off the sides. Small hands can't reach the scroll wheel comfortably. Neither is the user's fault.

😣

Grip Style Ignored

Claw grip users need less height. Fingertip grip users need narrower bodies. Palm grip users need rear extension. The standard shape serves none of them well.

🧠

Tactile Orientation Loss

Users with low vision or motor conditions rely on tactile landmarks to position their fingers correctly. A smooth uniform surface gives them nothing.

⏱️

Fatigue Over Time

Without support at the right points — thumb shelf, wrist rest, palm pad — extended use causes cumulative strain that builds up across workdays.

What Shape + Grip Solves

Five needs. One family.

Every module in this family addresses one of five functional needs identified from user research with people who have difficulty using standard mice.

01

Shape

Different outer shell profiles — sculpted palm rest, wide body, finger-guided surface — for different hand sizes and grip styles.

Modules: Standard Shell · Sculpted Palm · Finger Guide · Wide Body
02

Support

Wrist rest and palm pad attachments that provide a resting surface and reduce the effort of hovering the hand above the desk.

Modules: No Support · Flat Wrist Rest · Angled Palm Pad
03

Posture

Grip panels that change the effective width of the mouse, shifting hand posture and reducing ulnar deviation and wrist pronation.

Modules: Slim (3mm) · Standard (7mm) · Wide (14mm) · Ergonomic Contour
04

Fatigue

High-grip surfaces that reduce the grip force needed to control the mouse — allowing lighter contact and less muscular effort over time.

Modules: Smooth · Textured TPU · High-Grip Dots · Soft-Touch Foam
05

Tactile Markers

Raised dots, grooves, and finger position indicators that help users orient their hand without looking at the mouse.

Modules: None · Button Dot · Finger Grooves · Full Orientation Set

Getting Your Module

From configuration to your hand in 4 steps.

01

Configure

Use the configurator above to select your shell shape, grip width, texture, support, and tactile markers. The 3D preview shows exactly what you will receive.

02

Download

Download your STL package. It includes all the files for your configuration with print settings pre-embedded in the .3mf file.

03

Print or Order

Print at home if you have a printer, or upload to a service like Craftcloud, Shapeways, or a local print shop. PETG recommended. ~2–4 hours.

04

Attach

Snap the top case onto the magnetic plate. Slide grip panels onto the side rails. No tools needed. The mouse works immediately.
